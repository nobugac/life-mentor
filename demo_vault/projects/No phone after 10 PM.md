# No phone after 10 PM

**Type**: Behavior experiment (7-day cycles)
**Status**: Active (Week 2)
**Goal**: [[Sleep 7.5h+]]

## The practice
- After 10 PM: put phone on living room charger
- Use physical alarm clock
- Read or wind down without screens

## Why this works
- Reduces late-night scroll trap
- Signals bedtime to the brain
- Improves sleep onset and quality

## Progress
- Week 1 (Jan 27 – Feb 02): 5/7 days successful
- Week 2 (Feb 03 – Feb 07): 5/5 days successful
- Sleep score went from 72 → 85
